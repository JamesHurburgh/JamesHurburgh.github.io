Meet the team

We are a wife and husband team who have been balloon twisting since 2014 and loving every minute.

Talia Hurburgh

Talia has loved balloons since she was a child and has now been creating balloon sculptures for over 2 years. She is the main twister of Afternoon Balloon when she's not busy looking after our children.

James Hurburgh

When he's not goofing around with our children James enjoys making challenging balloon animals like his famous T-rex or crocodile.
