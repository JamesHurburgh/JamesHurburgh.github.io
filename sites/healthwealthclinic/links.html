Bowen South

Bowen Centre Brisbane South is dedicated to the art of Bowen Therapy. Bowen therapy is a very gentle form of natural healing involving only light moves applied to very specific points on the body. It is a, non-invasive, therapeutic technique suitable for a range of ailments and conditions.

Bowtech

The Bowen Technique (also known as Bowtech - the Original Bowen Technique and Bowenwork) is a dynamic system of muscle and connective tissue therapy that was developed by the late Tom Bowen in Geelong, Australia.
