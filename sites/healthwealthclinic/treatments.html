Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a system of complementary medicine in which fine needles are inserted in the skin at specific points along what are considered to be lines of energy (meridians), used in the treatment of various physical and mental conditions.

Acupuncture can:

  • Provides drug-free treatments
  • Very few side effects when provided by a qualified practitioner
  • Treats the underlying cause of Disease and illness as well as the symptoms
  • Provides a holistic approach to the treatment of disease and illness, linking body, mind and emotions

Assists in the prevention against disease and illness as well as the maintenance of general well-being Acupuncture is used for a variety of health conditions including

  • Gynaecological – painful menstruations, irregular, PMS etc
  • Fatigue and Insomnia
  • Colds, flu’s, ear infections, sinusitis and tinnitus
  • Digestive problems – indigestion and constipation
  • Migraines, headaches, stroke, Bell’s Palsy and Trigeminal Neuralgia
  • Pain relief
  • Musculoskeletal – fibromyalgia, sports injuries, arthritis etc
  • Depression, anxiety and PTSD
  • Pregnancy and Labour – infertility, morning sickness, labour pains, induction
